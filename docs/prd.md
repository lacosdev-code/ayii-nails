## 1. Introduction
**Product Name:** Ayii Nails Landing Page
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Status:** Live / Maintenance

### 1.1 Purpose
To provide a premium, high-conversion digital presence for "Ayii Nails," a home-service nail art business in Cilacap. The platform serves as a portfolio showcase and a direct booking funnel via WhatsApp.

### 1.2 Target Audience
- Women aged 18-45 in the Cilacap, Kroya, and Adipala regions.
- Students, professionals, and housewives looking for convenient, high-quality nail services.
- Users who value aesthetics and "Instagrammable" results.

## 2. Functional Requirements

### 2.1 Core Features
- **Hero Section:** High-impact visual introduction with a clear Call to Action (CTA) for booking.
- **Service Portfolio:** A gallery text/image showcase of nail art styles.
- **Coverage Checker:** Interactive list/pills to verify service availability in specific villages.
- **Testimonials:** Social proof section featuring authentic reviews with localized context.
- **WhatsApp Integration:** "Floating" and static CTAs that open WhatsApp with pre-filled booking messages.
- **FOMO Notifications:** Simulated "Recent Booking" popups to increase urgency and trust.

### 2.2 Technical Requirements
- **Performance:** Lighthouse score > 90 (Performance, Accessibility, SEO).
- **Responsiveness:** Mobile-first design, fully adaptive to Tablet and Desktop.
- **SEO:** Optimized meta tags, semantic HTML structure, and fast load times.

## 3. User Experience (UX) & Design
- **Aesthetic:** "Premium & Clean" (Apple-like). Minimalist, utilizing whitespace and elegant typography.
- **Color Palette:**
    - Primary: Ayii Gold (`#C9A27E`)
    - Background: Soft White/Beige (`#FAF9F7`)
    - Accents: Nude Pink (`#F3D6D6`)
- **Interactions:** Smooth spring-based animations (Framer Motion) for scrolling and hovering.

## 4. Future Roadmap (v2.0)
- [ ] Admin Dashboard for booking management.
- [ ] Dynamic verified reviews integration (Google Maps API).
- [ ] Blog section for nail care tips.

## 5. Success Metrics
- Click-through Rate (CTR) on "Booking via WhatsApp" buttons.
- Average Session Duration.
- Scroll Depth (Engagement with Service and Testimonial sections).
